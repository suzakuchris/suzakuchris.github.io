Character Sheet

The Lín family is not known for their exceptional martial arts, nor for their affiliation with any prestigious sects. They were masters of coin, lineage of wealthy merchants who happen to lend and funds many outer-sects in their immediate region. Although, not without any collaterals to back it up. As a result, they have had a lot of enemies. The head of the family has recently been slain in an altercation as the result, following several important figures. Effectively ending the family reputation as a merchant.

Lín Jīnzhū, the surviving daughter of the said Lín family, was left with nothing but the ashes, the remains of her family. Surviving through sheer luck, she had chosen to leave the comfort of living behind to walk on the path of vengeance, seeking the soul of the ones who orchestrated her family's ruin. Ever since the tragedy that robbed the future from her, she has been honing herself with any martial arts manual she can get her hands on, which brings her to the first manual of the Dragon's roar dagger (龙吟短剑 - Lóng yín duǎn jiàn), which belonged to a now dead martial artist who could not pay their debt.
